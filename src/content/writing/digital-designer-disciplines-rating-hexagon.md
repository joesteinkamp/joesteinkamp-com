## 

More focused on mid-senior tier designers to identify their distribution of knowledge with the ideal goal that you’re performing at high levels on all disciplines.

While it’s likely unrealistic for a single designer to maximize knowledge in all disciplines, it can establish developmental goals. It is also important for design managers to attempt to create a design team that as a collective can fill the diagram.
